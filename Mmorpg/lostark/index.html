LOST ARK
Lost Ark is a massively multiplayer online action role-playing game (MMOARPG) developed by Tripod Studio and Smilegate's game development subsidiary Smilegate RPG. It has been fully released in the Korean region on December 4, 2018. It's in open beta test (OBT) in the Russian region and it has started its closed beta test (CBT) registration in the Japanese region on June 25, 2020. The game is also very likely coming to European and North American regions sometime in 2021. The game has won six game awards in various categories at the Korea Game Awards 2019.
Gameplay
At the beginning of the game, you can choose one of five archetypes for the character-Monk, Warrior, Shooter, Mage, Assassin, each with several classes available. During the game, you can explore multiple locations and go through a fascinating story, completing tasks. For PvE fans, there are different types of dungeons available. Players are given the opportunity to pass hidden dungeons, use interactive objects, and fight bosses.

PvP supporters can fight in arenas. In addition, in the guild content, there are special maps for large-scale battles, vaguely reminiscent of the gameplay of MOBA games. There are also peaceful professions available in the game: you can become a collector or miner, harvest wood, hunt, fish, or even archaeology.
